Our Vision

Our Vision is Making Smile, Changing Life
Therefor our trustees took over many kind of missions to fullfill the vision of the Malar Foundation. Mainly our missions forces on children education, protecting child worker, disabled people and basic needy people.

Our Mission

Furthering our cause is the most important goal of our organization. We seek to support, empower, and provide high quality resources to our community in a safe and secure environment. We are here for the relief of poverty and the resettlement and the rehabilitation of children, because of the civil disturbances in Sri Lanka. Our mission fully forces POVERTY RELIEF, SHELTER, EDUCATION and MEDICINE.

Our Values

  • Improve the quality of life for the needy Children in the Sri Lanka.
  • Foster good community relationship between people of different races.
  • Provide a community centre to be used by all group of the community.
  • Advise the community on health and well-being through social, education leisure etc.
  • Assist community who are suffering from financial hardship, sickness & distress by providing advice, information, and welfare etc.
  • Work with Children and young adults through language class, mother & toddle group, providing advice to participating in community activities etc.

Our Integrity

We maintain 100% Transparency, deliver our projects at 0% Administration Cost. All financial transactions will be closely monitored by a minimum of two trustees on each payment being made by the Charity. Annual accounts will be published on the website. All monies raised, whether it be fundraising, individual donations or lobbying other organizations, will be dedicated purely to help the people we AID.
