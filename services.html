life is Changing the lives of Disabled
Every day, new life is changing the lives of disabled and terminally ill children across the Sri Lanka. The Malar Foundation offer a lifeline for families, providing life-changing and life-saving specialist equipment when and where it’s needed. Childhood cancers, birth defects, accidents, diseases, and infections can all cause disability. They are affecting children and their families every day.With your support, we can ensure more children get the best possible start in life and fulfil their potential.
Equipment Grant Services Providing families directly with essential and life changing equipment, such as beds, buggies, wheelchairs and more. Emergency Equipment Loans Service Offering free loan of equipment which can help children at significant risk of injury, or in urgent need. Play Therapy Pods Providing a free loan of specialist toys to disabled and terminally ill children, delivered direct to their door.
